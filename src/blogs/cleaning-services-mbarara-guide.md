## What Are Cleaning Services?

Cleaning services are professional solutions that help individuals, businesses, and institutions keep their spaces and property clean, organized, and hygienic. Instead of doing all the cleaning yourself, trained cleaners handle the work using proper tools, techniques, and cleaning products.

In Mbarara, cleaning services are commonly used by:

* Home owners
* Students in hostels
* Office managers
* Hotels and guest houses
* Airbnb hosts
* Shops and business owners

### Types of Cleaning Services in Mbarara

Cleaning services are not all the same. Here are the most common types available:

1. Residential Cleaning
This focuses on keeping homes clean and comfortable. It includes sweeping and mopping floors, dusting furniture, kitchen cleaning, bathroom sanitation, and general tidying of rooms.

2. Commercial Cleaning
Clean office spaces create a better working environment and improve productivity. Commercial cleaning includes desk and surface cleaning, floor cleaning and polishing, trash collection, restroom cleaning, and reception area maintenance. In busy towns like Mbarara, businesses often schedule cleaning early in the morning or after working hours.

3. Carpet, Sofa, and Mattress Cleaning
Over time, furniture collects dust, stains, and bacteria that regular cleaning cannot remove. This service includes deep carpet washing, sofa upholstery cleaning, as well as mattress stain and odor removal.

4. Laundry Services
Laundry services help with washing, drying, and ironing clothes professionally. They are useful for busy professionals, students, and families with large laundry loads. Services may include stain removal, folding, and careful fabric handling.

5. Fumigation and Pest Control
Pests like cockroaches, bed bugs, and mosquitoes are common in many homes and rentals. Fumigation services help to eliminate pests, prevent re-infestation, and improve hygiene and safety. This service is often needed in hostels, rentals, and older buildings.
