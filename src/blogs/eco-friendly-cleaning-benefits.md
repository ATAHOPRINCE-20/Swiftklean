For decades, the standard sign of a clean room was a strong smell of bleach or ammonia. However, modern research shows that these harsh chemicals pose significant health risks. Volatile organic compounds (VOCs) in traditional cleaners can trigger asthma, irritate eyes and skin, and affect indoor air quality.

Switching to eco-friendly cleaning products protects your family’s health. Green cleaners utilize natural ingredients like citric acid, vinegar, baking soda, and essential oils. They clean just as effectively as chemical counterparts without releasing toxic fumes or leaving hazardous residues.

If you have young children or pets who spend a lot of time on the floor, green cleaning is especially important. Babies and pets are highly sensitive to chemical residues left on carpets and tiles. Eco-friendly cleaning ensures they are playing in a safe, chemical-free environment.

Furthermore, green products are biodegradable, meaning they break down naturally without polluting water systems. Traditional cleaning chemicals wash down our drains and eventually end up in rivers and lakes, disrupting local ecosystems and wildlife.
