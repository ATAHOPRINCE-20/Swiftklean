## Why Do Cleaning Services Cost Different Amounts?

No two cleaning jobs are exactly alike. For example, cleaning a one-bedroom apartment takes much less time than cleaning a five-bedroom family home. Likewise, maintaining a small office is very different from carrying out a post-construction clean. Professional cleaning companies assess each project individually to provide a fair quotation based on the amount of work involved.

1. The Size of Your Property: The size of your home or business is one of the biggest factors that determines the cost of cleaning services in Mbarara. Larger spaces require more cleaning time, more cleaning products, more equipment and tools as well as more labour. As the property size increases, so does the amount of work required to do the cleaning.
2. The Type of Cleaning Service You Need: Different cleaning services require different levels of expertise and equipment. Some of the most common services include residential cleaning, office cleaning, carpet and sofa cleaning, laundry services, fumigation and pest control. Each service has different equipment, materials, and labour requirements, which can affect the overall price.
3. How Dirty Is the Property: A home that is cleaned every week usually takes much less effort than one that hasn't been cleaned for several months. Professional cleaners often spend additional time on heavy grease in kitchens, soap scum in bathrooms, tough stains on upholstery, construction dust, mould and mildew. The more work involved, the higher the cleaning cost may be.
4. How Often Do You Need Cleaning: Many clients choose regular cleaning instead of waiting until their property becomes heavily soiled. Common schedules include Weekly cleaning, Bi-weekly cleaning and Monthly cleaning. Regular cleaning helps maintain hygiene, reduces the need for intensive deep cleaning, and can be more cost-effective over time.
5. Special Cleaning Requests: Some cleaning projects include additional tasks that require extra time and attention. Examples include Interior window cleaning, refrigerator cleaning, oven cleaning, kitchen cabinet cleaning, mattress sanitisation as well as interior wardrobe cleaning. These services are normally discussed during the quotation process.
