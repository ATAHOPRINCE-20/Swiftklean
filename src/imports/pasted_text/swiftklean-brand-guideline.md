SwiftKlean Logo
Our logo is a symbol of speed, freshness, 
and precision.
The flowing motion element represents 
efficiency and movement, while the shirt icon 
reflects our core focus on laundry and 
garment care.
The clean lines and balanced design 
communicate professionalism and trust, while 
the teal color reinforces hygiene, clarity, and 
modern service standards.
Together, these elements create a strong and 
recognizable identity that reflects the values of 
SwiftKlean — fast, fresh, and reliable service.
Brand Guideline
Logo Variations
To maintain flexibility across different 
applications, SwiftKlean uses approved 
logo variations while preserving consistency 
and clarity.
Primary Logo (Full Color) – Used on light or 
white backgrounds for maximum visibility and 
brand recognition.
Black Logo – Used for monochrome 
applications such as official documents, 
embossing, or when color printing is 
unavailable.
White Logo (Reversed Version) – Used on 
dark or solid brand-color backgrounds to 
maintain contrast and legibility.
Wordmark– Used in situations where the icon 
has already appeared or when a cleaner, more 
minimal presentation is required to avoid 
repetition.

Logo Space
Logo Clear Space
To maintain clarity and visual impact, the 
SwiftKlean logo must always have adequate 
space around it.
The minimum clear space should be 10mm on 
all sides, measured from the outer edges of the 
logo. No text, images, borders, or graphic 
elements should enter this protected area.
Maintaining this space ensures the logo 
remains legible, balanced, and visually strong 
across all applications.

Typography is a core element of the SwiftKlean visual identity. It reinforces our 
professionalism, clarity, and modern service standards. Consistent use of type 
ensures our communication remains clean, structured, and recognizable.
Mono Sans Bold – Used for main headings and impactful statements.
Mono Sans SemiBold – Used for subheadings and highlighted text.
Mono Sans Regular – Used for body copy and general communication.
Mono Sans Light – Used for captions or supporting information.
Typography should always remain clean and readable. Avoid decorative or script 
fonts that compromise clarity and professionalism.
